We've reserved a block at two hotels in Seattle:

For most of our guests, we recommend reserving a room at Moxy Seattle Downtown, which is stylish, affordable, and about two blocks from the park we have reserved for our ceremony.

For our guests who would like a more luxurious stay, The Westin Seattle is a four-star hotel with a straight one-mile ride, via the South Lake Union Trolley, direct to Lake Union Park (you can also take a cab or walk).
